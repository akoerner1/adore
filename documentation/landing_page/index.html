About

Automated Driving Open Research (ADORe) Eclipse ADORe is a modular open source software library and toolkit for decision making, planning, control and simulation of automated vehicles.

We use ADORe to power motion and decision making in our research vehicles such as the FASCar and VIEWCar.

We provide interfaces to the simulation software of both CARLA and SUMO.

ADORe is ROS based.

ADORe is fully containerized with Docker.

Getting Started
